Why Sell Direct?

Selling traditionally works for some, but not everyone. Here's what makes working with a direct buyer different.

01

No Agent Commissions

Keep 100% of the sale price. Traditional sales typically cost 5-6% in agent fees—that's $15,000-$18,000 on a $300,000 home.

02

Sell As-Is

No need to make repairs, stage, or even clean. We buy properties in any condition—from pristine to project homes.

03

Close On Your Schedule

Need to close quickly? Or need more time? We work around your timeline, whether that's 7 days or 7 months.

04

No Showings or Open Houses

Skip the stress of constant showings, keeping your home spotless, and accommodating stranger's schedules.

05

Certainty Over Uncertainty

No waiting for buyer financing to fall through. We pay cash, which means no deal-killing appraisal issues or loan rejections.

06

We Cover Closing Costs

No hidden fees or surprise costs at closing. We handle the paperwork and cover standard closing costs.
